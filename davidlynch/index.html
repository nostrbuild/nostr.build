Tribute to David Lynch

David Lynch was more than just a film maker, he was an artist, with masterpieces that took you to another place and time. While most movies can be easily predicted, none of his creations had any known path, often leaving the viewer mezmorized, not only questioning the movie, but questioning their own life, existance, and reality.

There is no way to explain David Lynch in words, you have to watch his movies.
